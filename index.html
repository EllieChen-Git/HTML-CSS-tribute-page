Steve Jobs

The co-founder of Apple Inc and who changed the way we use mobiles.

Below is a timeline for Steve Jobs:

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, California
  • 1972 - Attended Reed College and dropped out the same year
  • 1974 - travelled through India
  • 1976 - Co-founded Apple with Wozniak
  • 1984 - Macintosh, the first mass-produced computer with a GUI
  • 1985 - Left Apple and founded NeXT
  • 1986 - Funded the spinout of The Graphics Group (later renamed Pixar)
  • 2003 - Was diagnosed with a pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor.
  • 2011 - Apple merged with NeXT and Jobs became the CEO of Apple again.
  • 2011 - Died at age 56 on October 5, 2011.

For more information about Jobs, please refer to Wikipedia.
